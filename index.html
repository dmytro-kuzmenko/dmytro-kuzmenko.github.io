Hi there!

I'm a Data Science Engineering Manager at LITSLINK and a Senior Lecturer at National University of Kyiv-Mohyla Academy. I'm currently doing my PhD in Time-efficient Reinforcement Learning in Robotics. I'm interested in improving inference and quality of mobile manipulation robotic systems based on vision-language models. I teach Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning for Computer Vision, and NLP Systems courses for grad and undergrad students, and I'm also a frequent speaker at AI-centered events in Ukraine.
