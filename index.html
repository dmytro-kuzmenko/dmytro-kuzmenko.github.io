Dmytro Kuzmenko

Head of AI @ OnlyMonster . PhD Candidate @ NaUKMA . Visiting PhD @ AIRlab, UniTo
Scholar GitHub LinkedIn X

I am a researcher working in robotics, machine learning, and human-robot interaction, with a focus on efficient and reliable intelligent systems. I have experience conducting research and supervising student work, alongside developing AI systems in applied settings.

My work bridges methodological research and real-world implementation, with an emphasis on reinforcement learning and physical intelligence. I am actively engaged in international collaboration and regularly present my work at conferences. In addition to my research, I am committed to teaching, mentoring, and fostering a collaborative research environment.

If you want to reach out, don't hesitate to send me an email.
