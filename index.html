Dmytro Kuzmenko

Head of AI @ OnlyMonster · PhD Candidate @ NaUKMA · Torino, Italy
Email Scholar GitHub LinkedIn X

I am an AI and robotics researcher focused on building efficient, adaptive systems at the intersection of reinforcement learning, robotics, foundation models, and human-robot interaction. As Head of AI at OnlyMonster, I also lead the development of generative and conversational AI products.

I am pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at NaUKMA, where I focus on resource-aware embodied intelligence through optimizing data, compute, and trust. In parallel, I work on human-robot interaction, with the goal of building AI systems that behave robustly, transparently, and in alignment with human expectations in real-world environments.

In my free time, I play shogi, collect vinyl records, and enjoy experimenting in the kitchen.

Interests: HRI, embodied AI, efficient RL, multi-task control, world foundation models.
